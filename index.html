Welcome to Explorable Explanations

The best way to understand complex concepts is to interact with them.

This suite of applets and interactive lectures provides a unique, hands-on approach to learning key principles in engineering and materials science.

Active Learning through Virtual Exploration

These applets are designed to help students:

  • Visualise abstract concepts
  • Experiment with variables in real-time
  • Develop an intuition for complex systems and models
  • Master "threshold concepts" crucial for advanced understanding of engineering topics
