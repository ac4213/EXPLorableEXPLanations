What We Offer

At Explorable Explanation, we believe that the best way to understand complex concepts is to interact with them. Our suite of applets provides a unique, hands-on approach to learning key principles in engineering and materials science.

Active Learning Through Virtual Exploration

Our applets are designed to help students:

  • Visualize abstract concepts
  • Experiment with variables in real-time
  • Develop intuition for complex systems
  • Master "threshold concepts" crucial for advanced understanding
