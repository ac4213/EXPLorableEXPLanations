üéÆ How to Play

Objective

Guess the biology-related word in 6 tries or less. Each word has a meaning provided in Chinese to help you.

Game Modes

Daily Mode: Play 5 words in sequence, increasing from 4 to 8 letters. Complete all 5 for your final score!

Random Mode: After completing the daily challenge, play unlimited random words.

Color Feedback

  • Green: Letter is correct and in the right position
  • Orange: Letter is in the word but wrong position
  • Gray: Letter is not in the word

Power-Ups

üîÑ Swap Try: Trade one attempt for a random power-up
