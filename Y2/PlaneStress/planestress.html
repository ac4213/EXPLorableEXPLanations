PLANE STRESS TRANSFORMATION

HOW TO PLAY:

Input the initial stress state using the input boxes in the lower-left corner.
The slider controls the orientation of the infinitesimal.
The Mohr's circle on the right shows the principal axis and stresses in blue.
The max shear axis and stress are shown in green.
The red line on the Mohr's circle represents the initial stress state.
The gray line represents the current orientation and its stress state.
